BurnRate · Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06/27/2026 · Contact: projecthub47@outlook.com

Short version

BurnRate does not collect personal data. Your game progress is saved locally on your device. We don't have a server that knows you exist.

What data we collect

None about you personally.

  • Your game save (producers owned, tokens, equity, achievements, unlocked vignettes, settings) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It never leaves the device.
  • We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel, nothing).
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not have an account system. You don't sign in.

Local notifications

BurnRate can send you a local reminder when offline tokens are close to capping out. These are local notifications — they are scheduled on your device by the app itself. No notification ever leaves your device. There is no push notification server.

Notifications are opt-in. You can disable them at any time in iOS Settings → BurnRate → Notifications, or in Android Settings.

Over-the-air updates

To deliver bug fixes and content updates without requiring you to download a new build from the App Store / Play Store, BurnRate uses Expo EAS Update. On launch, the app checks u.expo.dev for an updated JavaScript bundle.

This request includes a build identifier (so the server knows which bundle to send) but does not include any user-identifying information. Expo's privacy policy is at expo.dev/privacy.

Microphone & other sensitive permissions

BurnRate does not use the microphone, camera, contacts, calendar, location, motion sensors, or any other sensitive device feature. The app requests no such permissions.

The Android permission MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS is requested only to play in-game music and sound effects at the volume the player expects. It does not record or transmit audio.

Children's privacy

BurnRate is rated 4+ / Everyone and is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because we don't collect data at all.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data (we don't plan to), this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
