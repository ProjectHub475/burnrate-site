Common questions

I lost my progress. Saves are stored locally on your device. If you uninstalled the app, the save is gone — there is no cloud backup yet. Sorry.

The game is too easy / too hard. Pacing tunes ship via over-the-air updates regularly. If something feels off (a round that takes 30 minutes; a round that closes in 10 seconds), please email us — that's exactly the feedback we want.

I can't enable notifications. Open iOS Settings → BurnRate → Notifications → toggle Allow Notifications. On Android: Settings → Apps → BurnRate → Notifications.

Audio is silent even though Music is enabled. iPads don't have a hardware ringer switch; check Control Center for a "Mute" toggle. The game now plays background music regardless of the silent-mode toggle, but if you don't hear anything, try restarting the app.
